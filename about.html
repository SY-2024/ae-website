ABOUT US

Afro-European Business Services Ltd. is a company aimed at providing platforms to help ensure the smooth transition into UK life for migrant families, especially their children.

We offer support and encouragement for migrant children to help them stay connected with their native language and culture. They can also learn a European language or improve their English skills as well as understanding UK culture through history, politics and current affairs.

On a different front, we provide Amateur sports services for aspiring young athletes across all sports with focus on Tennis, Football and Athletics. We provide guidance and representation for the influx of a new generation of talent from Nigeria and other African countries.

OUR VISION

"Japa" is a term most of us are all too familiar with these days. Nigerians, Africans altogether are looking for greener pastures outside their country and continent. For many people, the United Kingdom is the place of choice to relocate to. This is where we, Afro-European Business Services Ltd. come in.
Afro-European Business Services is the company behind several different projects and partner-undertakings.

Our vision is to provide a platform, a place for people to come together under different aspects. A melting pot, if you so wish to call it, between Africa and Europe. Although, we wouldn't want to limit ourselves to these two continents alone. Wherever there are Africans, wanting to connect or come together, we aim to be in the middle of it, and make it happen.

Our projects are heavily focused on culture, heritage and most importantly the identity that comes from all that.

For those who know, we are providing a place to keep in touch with culture and traditions, and for those who want to learn, we proudly want to show off, what makes us who we are.

We are privileged to live in times of technological advancement, so we believe we should put this to good use. The world has become a global village, and we can stay connected to friends and family wherever we find ourselves in the world.

Why not also stay in touch with our culture, our traditions and our heritage. It is wonderful to see people coming together from different cultures, so we want to keep tapping into this. This can only foster harmony, as the more we know about each other, the more we can appreciate each other. As with so many things, it’s the differences that make us unique, but they can also bind us together.

The mission is to encourage those who leave their countries and continent to not forget where they have come from, and carry home in their heart, wherever they might find themselves.

We need to showcase the beauty and the richness of our continent and change stereotypes. We aim to get to a point, where we people want to give back to their respective communities and help create better lives for those, we left at home in Africa.
